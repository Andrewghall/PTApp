Meet Your Coach

Pedro Pereira

Head Coach & Founder, Elevate Gym - Quarteira, Algarve

Pedro is a Starting Strength Certified Practitioner who believes that barbell training is for everyone - regardless of age, background, or experience. Many of his clients started having never touched a barbell, and some were nervous about setting foot in a gym. That's completely okay.

As a certified practitioner of the Starting Strength method, Pedro coaches every repetition with patience, attention to your form, and a programme built entirely around you. His clients regularly see chronic pain fade away, improved performance in sports like golf and padel, and a quality of life they didn't think was possible. Whether you're 35 or 85, you'll be welcomed and supported from day one.

Training Philosophy

  • Your safety and comfort come first
  • Every client trains at their own pace
  • Programmes are tailored to your goals and abilities
  • Strength has no age limit
