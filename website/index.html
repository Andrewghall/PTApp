The Method

How It Works

The Starting Strength method is simple, hard, and effective. A proven system for building real-world strength.

01

Apply Stress

Load the five fundamental barbell movements with progressively heavier weight. Your body is challenged beyond its current capacity.

02

Recover

With proper nutrition, protein intake, and quality sleep between sessions, your body repairs and rebuilds stronger than before.

03

Adapt & Grow

Your body adapts to the stress by building muscle and increasing strength. This supercompensation is how you get stronger.

04

Progress

Add weight to the bar each session. This linear progression is the fastest, most effective way to build real-world strength.

The Foundation

The 5 Core Lifts

Master the fundamental barbell movements that build total-body strength, selected for their maximum muscle recruitment and longest effective range of motion.

Squat

The king of all exercises. Builds leg strength, core stability, and total-body power through the full range of motion.

Deadlift

The ultimate test of strength. Develops your entire posterior chain from grip to glutes in a single powerful movement.

Bench Press

Build upper-body pressing strength. Targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps with a heavy barbell load.

Overhead Press

True upper-body strength. Press weight overhead to build powerful shoulders, triceps, and core stability.

Box Squat

Develop explosive hip drive and perfect squat depth with controlled, powerful movements from a dead stop.
